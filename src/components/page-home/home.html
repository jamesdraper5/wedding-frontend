FAQs

THE VENUE, EVENTS, FOOD & DRINK

Do I need to book accommodation?

Rooms are €60 per person per night with breakfast included. Single rooms are €80 per person per night. Children 17 and under stay for free. Cots are available for smaller children.

How do I get to and from the venue?

We can help organise transfers to Borgo from airport through our wedding planner at a discounted rate. So please avail of this and let us know your flight details and contact us on 0851501145.

What food and drink is included?

Everyday

Your breakfast is included with your room (€60 per night pp / €80 pp for singles) paid upon your departure. You will need to cover your own snacks, lunch and drinks during the day.

Thursday Evening BBQ – 7.30pm

Food and drink is included for this event.

*** Friday Wedding Day – 3.00pm ***

Food and drink is included from the cocktail reception at 4pm, through the dinner until the cutting of the cake at 10/10.30pm. A cash bar opens after this time.

Saturday Evening Pizza Party - 7.00pm

The pizza party includes pizza!! Drink is not included this night but maybe we’ll already have had enough!

What is not on-site at the venue?

There is no ATM on site, however you will be able to add items to your room and pay upon departure.

There is no cigarette machine on site.

There is no shop on-site. It is a short drive to the local village for any supplies so feel free to bring along snacks. There is a bar and 2 restaurants on-site.

WHAT TO WEAR, HAIR, MAKE-UP

What should I wear to each event?

Thursday Evening BBQ – 7.30pm

Smart-Casual, Trendy and Cool! Get dressed up - You're on holidays!!! This welcome event is a chance for everyone to mingle and meet each other. It will be a sit down fun BBQ with drinks.

*** Wedding Day – 3.00pm ***

Style and glamour in a relaxed manner! Wear whatever you would wear to an Irish wedding but perhaps a little lighter. Maybe chinos and a shirt for the men and summer dresses for the ladies.

Saturday Evening Pizza Party - 7.00pm

Relax and wear your flats! This is a relaxed evening in the pub so feel free to be comfy and wear what you like!

Will I wear heels and facinators? (for the ladies)

Yes, of course! But please be aware, there is loose gravel and grass so get heel protectors if you are wearing stilettoes. Large heels and wedges are ideal… but I’ll be bringing my stilettoes!!

Should I bring a clothes iron?

There will be an iron or two made available by the venue for use in one of the communal areas.

Can I book a hairdresser appointment?

For those who have already requested, we have arranged for a few hairdressers and make-up artists to set up an area at the venue for styling hair on the morning of the wedding.

The price is approx €30-40 per person. We will let you know the time of your appointment when you check-in.

Hair - from €30
Make-up - from €25

OTHER QUESTIONS

What's the weather going to be like?

The weather should be fantastic in Rome in June, when the average temperature starts off at around 20°C and gradually climbs up to 23°C-24°C. However, there is always the possibility of rain, so be prepared!

Will my phone work?

You can use your Irish mobile in Italy but there will be some additional costs. Apparently, the costs are not huge like they used to be in bygone years, however, we recommend giving your phone provider a call to check what additional costs your will incur prior to going to Italy.
